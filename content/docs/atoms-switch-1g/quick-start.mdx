# Quick Start Guide

## Step 1 — Power On

Wire the switch into your wall box and restore power. The LED will pulse blue,
indicating it is in provisioning mode.

> ⚠️ **Safety first.** Always isolate the circuit at the breaker before wiring.
> See the full [Installation Guide](/docs/atoms-switch-1g/installation) for wiring diagrams.

## Step 2 — Connect to the Setup Hotspot

On your phone or laptop, connect to the Wi-Fi network:

```
Network: Atoms-Setup-XXXXXX
Password: atoms1234
```

## Step 3 — Open the Provisioning Page

Open your browser and go to:

```
http://192.168.4.1
```

The Atoms provisioning page will load. Enter your home Wi-Fi credentials and tap **Save**.

## Step 4 — Connect to Your Network

The device will reboot and join your Wi-Fi. The LED turns solid green when connected.

Find the device IP by checking your router's DHCP table, or use mDNS:

```
http://atoms-XXXXXX.local
```

## Step 5 — Test

Open the device web UI. Toggle the switch on and off. Done.
